Is it rude to only order soda at a restaurant?

Steven Brewer I've eaten at thousands of restaurants in all 50 states!
Answered 20h ago

Absolutely not! It happens every day in both restaurants and bars. As others have said, the profit margin on soda is astronomical! Where the possible rudeness comes in is how you are occupying the space in that restaurant while you are consuming your beverage! Restaurant owners need to turn tables and those tables need to generate a tab that makes them a profit. Servers normally survive on tips which are usually a percentage of the tab and your tab is going to be really low! So if you screw up either #1 or #2 or both and it’s usually both, your getting into rude territory.
